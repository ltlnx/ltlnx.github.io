The site

The site is a ground-up rework of my old site, which I preserved in the Links section. Reasons of revamping the site are on the homepage.

The visual style is inspired by absolute bare-bones sites like Dan Luu’s and Drew Devault’s. What I hope to achieve is a readable, lightweight and sturdy visual experience. As a side benefit, the site is perfectly Netsurf-able and Lynx-able.

If you’re using “dark mode” on your desktop or phone, however, you would see a dark theme powered by the Nord colorscheme. This gives us a modern and clean look without sacrificing readability.

The site is a WIP, so comments, feedback and suggestions on the content or the site are welcome.
